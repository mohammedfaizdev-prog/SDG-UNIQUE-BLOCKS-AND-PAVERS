Next Generation Equipments Built for Excellence

Our modern facility in Mysuru is built around automation, precision and zero-compromise quality control — combining German engineering with sustainable innovation.

  • Palfinger Truck Mounted Crane (Austria) — Heavy-duty handling with unmatched safety & efficiency.

  • Automated Material Handling System — Seamless operations for maximum productivity.

Building a Greener Tomorrow

Sustainability isn't a feature for us — it's how we run the plant. Sustainable by nature, strong by design.

  • Waste Reduction — Off-spec material reclaimed back into the process.
  • Energy Optimization — Efficient machinery and smart scheduling reduce energy per unit.
