Our Story

At SGD Unique Blocks & Pavers, we combine German engineering with sustainable innovation to deliver unmatched performance, precision, and productivity. We are Mysore's trusted manufacturer of premium concrete solid blocks, interlocking paver blocks, grass pavers, and kerb stones for residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects across Karnataka.

Our young team, state-of-the-art machinery, and tight quality discipline mean every block leaving our yard in Kumar Beedu, Bogadi, Mysuru meets the same uncompromising standard. We believe the future of construction is greener, smarter, and more precise — and we are here to help architects, contractors, and homeowners build it with the best concrete blocks and pavers in Mysore.

  • Sustainable by Nature — Eco-friendly manufacturing with low-carbon mix designs and responsible sourcing of materials
  • Strong by Design — Precision-engineered concrete blocks and pavers built to last with German technology
  • Trusted in Mysore — Serving Karnataka with premium quality solid blocks, paver blocks, kerb stones, and grass pavers

Our purpose-built facility in Kumar Beedu, Bogadi, Mysuru, is equipped with state-of-the-art German technology including fully automatic block machines, hydraulic pan mixers, and Palfinger truck-mounted cranes from Austria. We serve all of Karnataka including Bengaluru, Mandya, Hassan, Chamarajanagar, Kodagu, and Tumakuru.
