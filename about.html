Our Story

At SGD Unique Blocks & Pavers, we combine German engineering with sustainable innovation to deliver unmatched performance, precision, and productivity. Our young team, state-of-the-art machinery and tight quality discipline mean every block leaving our yard meets the same uncompromising standard.

We are a newly started company committed to delivering high-quality blocks and pavers with strength, durability, and trust. We believe the future of construction is greener, smarter and more precise — and we are here to help architects, contractors and homeowners build it.

  • Sustainable by Nature — Eco-friendly manufacturing with low-carbon mix designs
  • Strong by Design — Precision-engineered concrete products built to last
  • German Engineering — Fully automated facility with world-class machinery in Mysuru

Our purpose-built facility in Kumar Beedu, Bogadi, Mysuru, is equipped with state-of-the-art German technology including fully automatic block machines, hydraulic pan mixers, and Palfinger truck-mounted cranes from Austria.
