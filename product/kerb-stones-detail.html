Description

Concrete kerb stones are essential edge restraints designed to provide clean separation between different surfaces while preventing soil erosion, maintaining boundaries, and enhancing overall landscape aesthetics. Also known as curbstones or edge restraints, these durable elements are indispensable for defining and protecting paved areas in Mysore and across Karnataka.

Available in standard sizes with 6 color variants, our kerb stones are manufactured using German technology for superior strength, precision fit, and exceptional durability in all weather conditions. They serve as a vital component in road construction, garden landscaping, and urban infrastructure projects by containing paved surfaces and preventing the encroachment of soil or vegetation onto driveways and pathways.

Perfect for: Road edges, garden borders, pathway edges, parking lot perimeters, landscaping projects, tree surrounds, planters, and commercial plazas in Mysore, Bengaluru, Mandya, Hassan, and all of Karnataka.
