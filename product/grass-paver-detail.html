Description

Sustainable permeable paving solution that allows grass to grow through while providing structural support for vehicle parking. The open-cell design promotes natural drainage, reduces runoff, and creates green spaces. Engineered with German technology for superior strength and durability. Building a Greener Tomorrow.

  • Material: High-grade permeable concrete
