Description

Grass paver blocks, also known as eco pavers or permeable pavers, are a sustainable and innovative paving solution that combines structural integrity with environmental benefits. These open-cell concrete blocks allow grass to grow through the voids, creating a green, permeable surface that supports vehicle traffic while promoting natural drainage.

The unique cellular design prevents soil compaction, allows water to infiltrate naturally, and reduces stormwater runoff, making them an ideal choice for environmentally conscious projects in Mysore and across Karnataka. Available in multiple sizes and 6 color variants, our grass pavers are manufactured with German technology for superior strength, durability, and precision fit.

Perfect for: Parking lots, grass pathways, fire access roads, eco-friendly driveways, parks, sports facilities, and rain gardens in Mysore, Bengaluru, Mandya, Hassan, and all of Karnataka.
