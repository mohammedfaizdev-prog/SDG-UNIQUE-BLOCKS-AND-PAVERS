Description

Precision-engineered solid concrete blocks for strong, durable wall construction. Available in 4", 6", and 8" sizes with 6 color variants. Ideal for load-bearing walls, partition walls, and foundation work. Engineered with German technology for superior strength and dimensional accuracy.
